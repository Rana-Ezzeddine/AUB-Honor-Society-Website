About the Society

The AUB Honor Society stands as a testament to the American University of Beirut's unwavering commitment to academic excellence, leadership, and community service.

Established to recognize and celebrate students who exemplify these core values, the society fosters a culture of integrity, scholarly achievement, and civic responsibility.

Through various initiatives, the society aims to inspire members to contribute meaningfully to both the university and the broader community.

Eligibility Criteria

Academic Excellence: Students must maintain a high GPA, typically within the top percentile of their class, reflecting consistent scholarly achievement.

Leadership Qualities: Active participation in extracurricular activities, student organizations, or community service projects that showcase leadership and initiative.

Adherence to AUB's Code of Conduct: Demonstrating integrity, responsibility, and respect within the university community.

Membership Benefits

Recognition: Official acknowledgment during university ceremonies and events.

Networking Opportunities: Access to a network of distinguished alumni and professionals.

Leadership Development: Opportunities to engage in leadership roles within the society and university.

Career Advancement: Enhanced prospects through the society's connections and resources.
